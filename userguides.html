Everything you need to install, maintain, and troubleshoot your Luxury Bidet.

Full Length Installation Guide

Learn how to install your Luxury Bidet system step by step. Begin by turning off the water supply, then connect the T-valve and water hose. Secure the seat, reattach the water supply, and test for leaks. Each model includes a quick-connect adapter and an illustrated setup guide for easy installation.

Maintenance Guide

Keep your bidet running like new. Check the hose and valve every few months to ensure there are no leaks. Replace the water filter annually and make sure no buildup occurs on the nozzle. For heated seats, verify that power connections are secure and temperature settings respond normally.

Cleaning Tutorial

Wipe the seat and nozzle with a soft, damp cloth using mild soap or a pH-neutral cleaner. Avoid bleach or alcohol-based cleaners. Activate the nozzle self-cleaning feature weekly to prevent buildup. For a deep clean, remove the seat and gently rinse it under warm water.

Troubleshooting Tips

If your bidet is not spraying, check the water valve and pressure settings first. Make sure the nozzle is not clogged, and that power is connected if it’s an electric model. For persistent issues, consult our online support for model-specific error codes and reset instructions.
